jwst_example.py ¶

Routines for generating synthetic JWST observations of planet-planet occultations in TRAPPIST-1.

scripts.jwst_example.Stacked_bc(N=4)¶

Simulate N stacked exposures of b occulting c in the MIRI F1500W filter.

scripts.jwst_example.Stacked_bc_all_filters(N=4)¶

Simulate N stacked exposures of b occulting c observed in all MIRI filters.

scripts.jwst_example.Triple_bc()¶

Simulate an observation of a triple occultation of TRAPPIST-1 c by b with JWST MIRI at 15 microns.
